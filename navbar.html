Round Robin Tournaments

How It Works

Round Robin tournaments follow the setup of a typical 5-round, single-elimination tournament with slight variations:

1. Teams have 24 hours to complete each round. Rounds may be played at any time within the 24 hour limit.

2. There does not need to be 32 teams entered in order for the tournament to begin, and there is no limit on how many teams can be entered at once. Teams simply join in at round one, at any time.

3. Teams play against other teams in the same round. (i.e a team in round one can only play another team in round one, and so on, and so forth.
